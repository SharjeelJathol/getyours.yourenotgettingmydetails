What it's not for

We're only looking out for you

  • Signing up to websites
  • Subscribing to spam
  • Purchasing goods online

It's up to you if you'd like any website or any company to hold your @yourenotgettingmydetails.com email address but be careful, do it enough times and you'll be building an online profile that can be used to identify who you are. 

Spam emails are more than just annoying, they're actually used to collect your data. Most businesses these days use clever scripts embedded within an email body that can reveal to them several identifying characteristics such as your location, your browser cookies and even just the fact that you've opened the email can reveal who you are as it all goes towards building an online profile of you.

There's no better way to allow a business or organisation to build a profile of you than to buy something, anything. What you purchases, how much you paid, what time it was, what browser you used, what other cookies you have and of course what email address you used is all put together to build a profile and is then shared with anyone who's willing to pay for ti, it's built up each time it's sold on and before you know it, you're on the receiving end of targeted ads and receiving spam calls from someone who actually knows your name.
